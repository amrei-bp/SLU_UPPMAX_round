Compute & Storage Resources for SLU

Access to Compute and Storage Resources

This page provides an overview of how to apply for pay-as-you-use compute and storage resources at UPPMAX, what systems are available, how to manage your allocation, and where to get support.

How to Apply

Step 1: Apply for a NAISS/SUPR Account

Step 2: Apply for Resources via "SLU Local 2026"

Step 3: Access After Approval

Available Systems

Through the SLU round, the following resources can be applied for at UPPMAX:

  • Pelle: compute resources. Can be applied for individually, or with additional storage on Gorilla.
  • Gorilla: active project storage. Can be applied for individually, or in an application together with a compute project on Pelle.
  • Lutra: long term storage. Needs to be applied for in a separate application from Pelle and/or Gorilla.

Duration of Allocations

Allocations on Pelle and Gorilla can be requested for up to a year at a time. If needed, they can be renewed for up to another year at a time, for as long as you need the resources. You will receive an email reminder before your current allocation expires.

Once no longer needed, you can simply remove your data and not request a prolongation of your project when it is running out.

Storage allocations on Lutra will be granted for four years at a time.

Example: Annual cost for a small project

Based on the UPPMAX price list.

Storage on Gorilla

  • Data volume: 2 TB
  • Price: 0.1 SEK per TB-hour
  • Calculation:
    2 TB × 365 days × 24 hours/day × 0.1 SEK/TB-hour
  • Annual storage cost: 1,752 SEK

Compute on Pelle

  • Compute use: 24,000 core-hours
  • Price: 0.1 SEK per core-hour
  • Calculation:
    24,000 × 0.1 SEK
  • Annual compute cost: 2,400 SEK

Total annual cost

4,152 SEK

Billing

Billing follows the Uppmax pricing model, but will be send out, and paid to SLU-IT.

You will be invoiced for the resources your are using, not for the ones you have allocated to your project.
