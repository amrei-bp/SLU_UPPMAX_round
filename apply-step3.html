Step 3 – Apply for a UPPMAX Account

Apply for a UPPMAX Account

Once your project has been approved in SUPR, your allocation on Gorilla, Pelle, and/or Lutra becomes active.
To log in to these systems, you must also have a UPPMAX user account. This account is separate from SUPR and is required for accessing the compute clusters operated by UPPMAX.

Why You Need a UPPMAX Account

The UPPMAX systems (Gorilla, Pelle, Lutra) are operated by Uppsala University.

SUPR is used to manage the resources applications and allocations, but you still need a local UPPMAX user account so you can:

  • log in via SSH
  • access project directories
  • run jobs on the clusters
  • use software modules and storage areas

How to Apply for a UPPMAX Account

  1. Log into SUPR here: https://supr.naiss.se, using the same identity you used for SUPR.
  2. In the left hand menu, click Accounts.
  3. Under the heading Account Requests, click Request account.

Approval and Activation

  • UPPMAX staff will approve your request and create your user account.
  • Once created, you will receive an email with login details (including your username).
  • After activation, you can log in to Gorilla, Pelle, or Lutra immediately.

After You Have Your Account

When your UPPMAX account is active, you can:

  • connect to the clusters via SSH
  • access your project directory
  • start running jobs and workflows
  • use software modules and storage areas
  • monitor resource usage
